# Poets United

A simple space to share poetry. Post your finished pieces, tender drafts, and small truths — anonymously or not.

## Stack

- **Next.js** — frontend and API routes
- **Supabase** — database (poems storage)
- **Tailwind CSS** — styling


## Features

- Browse poems submitted by the community
- Submit your own poem via the upload form
- Post anonymously or with your name
- Line breaks are preserved as written


## Motivation

This project was inspired by my love for poetry and the joy of sharing it with others. I wanted to create a space where I could share my own poetry with the world.
The goal of Poets United is to be a place where people can come together to share their love for poetry.
I hope you enjoy it!
